    • Setup

    Always refer to our guides on the NExT Lab Knowledge Base.

    Space and Sensor Considerations:

    • Clear space: Free of obstacles
    • Adequate Space: At least 1.5m x 2m
    • Sensors Specs: 120° viewing angle in all directions
    • Sensor Locations: Diagonally, up to 5m apart
    • Sensor Heights: Above head height, angled 30°-45° down
